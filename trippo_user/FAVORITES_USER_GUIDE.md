## â“ FAQ

### Q: How many favorites can I have?
**A:** Unlimited! Add as many as you want.

### Q: Are favorites saved across devices?
**A:** Yes! They're stored in your Firebase account and sync automatically.

### Q: Can I rename favorites?
**A:** Currently no, but coming soon! For now, the place name is saved automatically.

### Q: What happens to favorites if I log out?
**A:** They're saved to your account. When you log back in, they'll be there!

### Q: Can I share favorites with friends?
**A:** Not yet, but this feature may be added in the future.

### Q: Why isn't my favorite showing up?
**A:** Make sure:
- You're logged in
- You've switched to the Favorites tab
- The Firestore index has finished building (takes 5-10 min initially)

### Q: Can I organize favorites into folders?
**A:** Not yet, but they auto-sort by usage - most-used appear first!
